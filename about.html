About Amrita

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is a multi-disciplinary, research-intensive, private university, educating a vibrant student population of over 30,000 by 2000+ strong faculty. Accredited with the highest possible ‘A++’ grade by NAAC, Amrita offers more than 300 UG, PG, and Ph.D. programs across various disciplines...

With its extensive network of nine campuses across India, covering 1200+ acres and 100 lakh sq ft of built-up space, Amrita stands among India's top-tier private universities, known for its academic excellence and social commitment.

Our Mission

Education for Life

Amrita believes in education that imparts essential human values. Rooted in Dharma, Karuna, and Shraddha, our students develop compassion, patience, and a strong inner foundation to create a better world.

Compassion Driven Research

Our research addresses poverty, illness, and environmental degradation. By transforming compassion into action, Amrita drives innovation with global impact and societal relevance.
